La Conner, Washington

Welcome to La Conner!

La Conner, Washington is a small waterfront town located in Skagit County, in between the Swinomish Channel and beautiful farmland. It offers breathtaking views of the Cascade Mountains while being surrounded by tidal flats and estuaries.

Things to Do

  • Rainbow Bridge

    The Rainbow Bridge was built in 1957, and is now a National Landmark. You can walk aross the bridge and watch the boats pass below.

  • Museum of Northwest Art

    The Museum of Northwest Art contains a wide varity of art and features exclusively Pacific Northwest artists.

  • Cedar Spirit Wheel

    Visit the hand-carved cedar Spirit Wheel and give it a spin! Located in front of Maple Hall, and carved by local Swinomish artist and wood-carver, Kevin Paul.

  • Skagit Valley Tulip Festival

    Explore the beautiful and vibrant tulip fields located in the Skagit Valley Tulip Festival.

Information

Population: 965

Geography: Small waterfront town located between the Swinomish Channel and beautiful farmland.

Weather: Mild, rainy winters; cool, dry summers; coastal maritime climate.
